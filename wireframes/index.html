Wireframes and User Stories

Portfolio Site

  1. Future Employer/Client
    • employer that i've summited a resume to
    • easy access to art work
    • would be coming to the home page first
    • interested in layout of website
    • interested in how well the website functions
    • interactive elements
    • looking for contact information
    • might want to know your background
  2. Friends/Families
    • interested in seeing art work
    • easy access to art work
    • less interested in design and layout (not as conscious about design)
    • place to write positive comments/feedback
    • coming to home page first
    • would want to know contact info
    • email, phone number
    • any works of art for sale?
  3. Random Web Surfers
    • could be landing on any page within the site
    • easy access back to home page
    • clearly defined nav bar
    • elements to keep them from clicking off your page
    • interactive design, typography, layout, etc.
    • quick, easy access to art work and contact info
    • clearly defined headers
    • easy to recognize as a portfolio site

Restaurant Site

  1. Young Couple
    • Casual date
    • Wants to make a dinner reservation
    • Nav bar easy recognizable
    • Reservations, Contact Info, About the Rest.
    • hours
    • menu
    • drink menu
    • Directions, address, phone number
    • modern, hip, "young" design
    • images
    • interactive
  2. Family
    • family dinner to celebrate a birthday
    • menu for parents
    • menu for children
    • dessert menu/options for birthday desserts
    • any special discounts
    • dinner specials
    • prices
    • contact info/about/location
    • hours
    • images of the space/family friendly dinning
    • playful design/interactive/welcoming
  3. Older Couple
    • celebrating wedding anniversary
    • menu with prices
    • senior discounts
    • dinner specials
    • very simple design
    • easy access to nav bar
    • simple nav bar
    • contact info, about, location
    • reservations
    • minimal to no interactive design
    • a few images of restaurant and dishes
    • special amenities for celebrations
