Serves 4

Mixed Fruit Parfait

  • In four parfait glasses or serving dishes, layer the pineapple, raspberries, yogurt, banana and raisins.
  • Sprinkle with almonds.
  • Serve immediately.

What we'll need

  • 2 cups pineapple chunks
  • 1 cup raspberries
  • 8 oz vanilla yogurt
  • 1 cup sliced ripe banana
  • 1/2 cup raisins
  • 1/4 cup sliced almonds
