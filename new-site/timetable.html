Train Timetable

14"x8.5"

A redesign of an existing train timetable which fits within an 8.5"x14" (or 14" by 8.5") format. Limitations of two type families, and black + one spot color. This was as much an information design project as a technical exercise to increase skills on how to best present a large volume of time-related data in a small space, in a clear and easy-to-understand way.
